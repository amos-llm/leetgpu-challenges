Implement a Grouped General Matrix Multiplication. The input matrix A of shape M_total × K is partitioned into G contiguous row groups whose boundaries are given by the cumulative array group_offsets of length G + 1. Each group g has its own weight matrix B[g] of shape K × N. For every group, multiply the rows assigned to it by that group's weight matrix and write the result into the corresponding rows of C.

Formally, for every group g in 0, 1, ..., G-1, let s = group_offsets[g] and e = group_offsets[g+1]. Then C[s:e] = A[s:e] × B[g]. A group may be empty (s == e), in which case no rows are written for that group.

This pattern is the core building block of Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) inference: after a router has dispatched tokens to experts, each expert holds a variable number of tokens that all share the same weight matrix. A single grouped GEMM computes every expert's projection in one launch.

Implementation Requirements

  • Implement the solve function; do not change its signature.
  • External libraries are not permitted.
  • Write the result into C.

Examples

Example 1: G = 2, M_total = 4, K = 2, N = 3, group_offsets = [0, 2, 4]

\[ A = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 \\ 3 & 4 \\ 5 & 6 \\ 7 & 8 \end{bmatrix},\quad B[0] = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 & 1 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 \end{bmatrix},\quad B[1] = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 1 & 0 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 \end{bmatrix} \]

\[ C = \begin{bmatrix} 1 & 2 & 1 \\ 3 & 4 & 3 \\ 11 & 11 & 6 \\ 15 & 15 & 8 \end{bmatrix} \]

Group 0 occupies rows [0, 2) of A, so C[0:2] = A[0:2] × B[0]. Group 1 occupies rows [2, 4), so C[2:4] = A[2:4] × B[1].

Constraints

  • 1 ≤ G ≤ 64 (number of groups)
  • 1 ≤ M_total ≤ 16,384 (total rows across all groups)
  • 1 ≤ K, N ≤ 4,096
  • group_offsets[0] = 0, group_offsets[G] = M_total, and group_offsets is non-decreasing
  • A group may be empty (group_offsets[g+1] = group_offsets[g]); in that case nothing is written for that group
  • A, B, and C are 32-bit floats; group_offsets is 32-bit integer
  • All tensors are stored on the GPU
  • Performance is measured with G = 8, M_total = 8,192, K = 1,024, N = 2,048
